As a matter of fact. other conditions being equal (that is. a partially static

0n the other hand. a high added value. in proportion to sales. provides greater

Considered by itself. that is. as an absolute value. it is not very indicative.

However. it is an important factor if related to other factors (sales. added

Finance charges. These charges were calculated at the net value of the finan-
